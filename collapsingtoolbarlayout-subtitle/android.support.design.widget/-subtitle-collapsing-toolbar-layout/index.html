SubtitleCollapsingToolbarLayout

open class SubtitleCollapsingToolbarLayout : FrameLayout

A Toolbar wrapper with subtitle support. It is identical to CollapsingToolbarLayout and shares the same behavior and attributes, in addition to its own attributes defined in R.styleable.SubtitleCollapsingToolbarLayout.
